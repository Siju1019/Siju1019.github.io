Pediatrician

Description

Diagnose, treat, and help prevent diseases and injuries in children. May refer patients to specialists for further diagnosis or treatment, as needed.

Average Salary

$175,310

Employers

  • Outpatient Care Systems
  • General Medical and Surgical Hospitals
  • Specialty (except Psychiatric and Substance Abuse) Hospitals
  • Family Clinics

Related Careers

  • Healthcare Social Workers:Provides support and services to groups, individuals, or families that are challenged to cope with terminal, acute, or chronic illness.
  • Physician Assistants:Helps the physician with small jobs
  • Family Medicine Physicians:Offers expert medical care to adults and kids.
